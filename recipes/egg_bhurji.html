Egg Bhurji

Description

Learn to make Egg Bhurji aka Indian spiced scrambled eggs with my simple recipe. Egg Bhurji is the easiest you can make any time & just takes about 20 mins.

Made with basic ingredients, this beginnersâ€™ recipe is sure to impress you with tons of flavors & deliciousness. It goes well with bread, pav, Chapati, roti, Poori or even with plain steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Coriander Leaves

Steps

  1. Heat the pan with oil
  2. Saute Eggs, Tomatoes, Onions
  3. Add spices
  4. Sprinkle leaves
  5. Enjoy!
